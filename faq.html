HOW DO I JOIN QEEDA TOURNAMENTS?

Please follow these simple steps to join the Qeeda Tournaments!
1 - Go to the Tournaments tab.
2 - Select a Tournament(FREE FIRE, LD etc.) of your choice then it will redirect to Tournament detail page.
3 - Now click on Join Tournament button to continue.
4 - Every Tournament let you win Cash, Coins and require an entry fee, for which we deduct payment form Qeeda wallet.
5 - Once the Tournament starts you have to play the game( FREE FIRE, LUCKY DICE etc.) which is mentioned in Tournament. Final score, rankings and winners are declared after the end of the tournament.

HOW DO I PLAY THE FANTASY CRICKET GAME?

Please follow these simple steps to play the Fantasy Cricket game!
1 - Select an upcmoing match and then it will redirect you to Fantasy list pages.
2 - Select a Fantasy of your choice then it will redirect to create team page once you clicked on Create Team button.
3 - Select your Fantasy Squad of 11 Players within the credits of 100 from all the Players in the particular match.
4 - Now Join the Fantasy. Fantasy let you win Cash, Coins and require an entry fee, for which we deduct payment form Qeeda wallet.
5 - Once the Live match starts, your Fantasy Squad starts earning points on the basis of actual performance of the Players selected by you. Final points, rankings and winners are declared after the end of the match.

CAN I MAKE CHANGES IN THE SQUAD AFTER I ENTER A FANTASY?

Yes, you can make changes in your Squad until the Fantasy is closed. Once the Fantasy is closed you cannot make changes in your Squad. The Fantasy is closed before the first bowl of the official match is bowled or with the official match beginning time.
